Frequently Asked Questions

How long does taxidermy take?

Typical turnaround time is 6-12 months depending on the type of mount and our current workload. We'll provide an estimate when you drop off your trophy.

Do you process during hunting season?

Yes! We're open throughout hunting season with extended hours to accommodate your harvest. Call ahead for current wait times.

What should I do immediately after my harvest?

Keep the animal cool and get it to us as quickly as possible. For taxidermy, avoid cutting the cape. Contact us for specific field care instructions.

Do you offer pickup services?

Pickup services may be available for large game or special circumstances. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
