Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own hide?

Absolutely! We work with customer-supplied hides from your harvest. We can also supply hides if you don't have your own.

How long does hide tanning take?

Typical turnaround time varies by project size and our current workload. Contact us for current estimates.

What can you make from hide scraps?

Leftover leather from larger projects becomes keychains, hat patches, wallet inserts, lanyards, and other small goods. Nothing goes to waste!

Do you repair old hide rugs?

Yes! We offer re-backing, stitch repair, edge fixes, and restoration services for hide rugs and leather goods.
