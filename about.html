Our Story

THGS Primitive Hideworks (formerly THGS Field To Freezer) is a family-run business in Dansville, NY, founded on a passion for traditional leather working and hide crafting. We understand that every hide has a story and represents a connection to the land and the hunt.

We combine time-tested hide tanning and leather working techniques to create quality, handcrafted pieces. Whether you're bringing your own hide from your harvest or selecting one of ours, we treat every project with care and respect for the material and the craft.
