About THGS Field To Freezer

Your trusted partner in taxidermy and game processing

Our Story

THGS Field To Freezer is a family-run business right here in Dansville, NY, founded on a passion for the outdoors and respect for the hunting tradition. We understand that every trophy represents hours of preparation, patience, and the thrill of the harvest.

We combine time-tested taxidermy techniques with reliable processing methods to deliver quality results. Whether you're looking to mount a trophy buck or process your harvest into good cuts for your family, we treat every project with care and respect.
