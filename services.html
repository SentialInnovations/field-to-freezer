Our Services

Expert taxidermy and game processing for hunters who demand the best

Taxidermy

Professional Taxidermy Services

Transform your trophy into a lasting memory with our expert taxidermy services. Each mount is carefully crafted to capture the natural beauty and spirit of your harvest.

What We Offer

Shoulder Mounts

Classic shoulder mounts that showcase your trophy with lifelike detail and expert positioning.

Full Body Mounts

Complete full-body displays that capture the full majesty of your harvest in action poses.

European Mounts

Clean, elegant skull mounts that highlight the natural bone structure and trophy antlers.

Habitat Dioramas

Custom displays with natural habitat elements that tell the complete story of your hunt.

Rug Mounts

Professional rug mounting services for bears, large cats, and other trophy game.

Bird Taxidermy

Waterfowl and upland bird mounts with detailed feather work and natural positioning.

Our Process

1

Consultation

We discuss your vision, pose preferences, and desired mounting style for your trophy.

2

Preparation

Expert skinning, tanning, and preparation ensure the highest quality foundation for your mount.

3

Mounting

Skilled artisans create lifelike mounts with attention to anatomical accuracy and natural appearance.

4

Finishing

Final detailing, habitat work, and quality assurance before your trophy is ready for display.

Processing

Game Processing Services

Professional game processing in our USDA-approved facility. We handle your harvest with care, ensuring premium quality cuts and proper storage.

Processing Options

Standard Processing

Complete breakdown into steaks, roasts, and ground meat. Vacuum sealed and frozen.

Custom Cuts

Specify your preferred cuts, thickness, and packaging to meet your family's needs.

Sausage Making

Premium sausages in a variety of flavors. Made fresh with your choice of seasonings.

Jerky

Hand-cut, marinated, and dried to perfection. Multiple flavor options available.

Specialty Items

Smoked products, brats, summer sausage, and other specialty preparations available.

Caping for Taxidermy

Expert field caping and preparation for taxidermy while processing your meat.

Processing Standards

✓

USDA Approved Facility

Our processing facility meets all federal standards for cleanliness and food safety.

✓

Individual Processing

Your harvest stays separate throughout processing - you get back only your own meat.

✓

Quick Turnaround
