Hides & Rugs

Hide Rugs, Wall Hangings & Tanned Hides

Transform your harvest into a beautiful hide rug or wall hanging that tells your story. We also offer tanned hides ready for your own projects. You can supply your own hide from your harvest, or we can provide quality hides.

What We Offer

Hide Rugs

Full hide rugs professionally tanned and finished. Perfect for displaying your trophy or adding rustic character to your space.

Wall Hangings

Hide wall hangings that showcase the natural beauty of the hide. Great for cabins, lodges, or home decor.

Tanned Hides

Quality tanned hides ready for your own creative projects. Soft, supple, and properly finished.

Customer Hide Service

Bring your own hide from your harvest and we'll tan and finish it to your specifications.

Custom Goods

Custom Leather Goods & Small Items

Handcrafted leather goods made to order. From custom gear to small accessories, every piece is unique. We can work with hides you provide or use our own quality materials. Leftover scraps from larger projects become keychains, patches, and other small items - nothing goes to waste.

What We Make

Custom Gear

Knife sheaths, gun slings, belt pouches, and other functional leather gear made to your needs.

Small Goods

Wallets, belts, card holders, and other everyday carry items crafted from quality leather.

Keychains & Lanyards

Hat Patches

Custom leather hat patches with tooling, branding, or simple designs.

Wallet Inserts

Repairs

Repairs & Restoration Services

Don't let a damaged hide rug or leather item go to waste. We offer repair and restoration services to bring your pieces back to life. From re-backing old rugs to fixing torn edges and broken stitching, we can help preserve your treasured items.

Repair Services

Re-back Rugs

Old backing deteriorating? We can remove old backing and install new, quality backing material.

Stitch Repair

Fix torn seams, loose stitching, or add reinforcement to weak areas.

Edge Fixes

Repair torn or damaged edges on hide rugs and leather goods. Restore clean, finished edges.

Leather Restoration

Bring dried out or damaged leather back to life with proper conditioning and care.

Why Repair?

✓

Preserve Memories

Keep heirloom pieces and trophy hides in the family for generations.

✓

Cost Effective

Repairs are often more affordable than replacing a quality hide or leather piece.

✓

Expert Assessment

We'll evaluate your piece and let you know what's possible and what it will take.
